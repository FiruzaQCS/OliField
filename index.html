    About Us

    The Oilfield Engineering & Supply Services LLC (OESS) was incorporated in 2020 by highly qualified professionals with the purpose of provision
    of engineering and supply services in Oil and Gas sector as well as other industries. OESS founders are pioneering new opportunities and
    bringing value to its customers more than 15 years. The company experiences vast growth within the Oil and Gas Industry, delivering trusted and
    excellent results with high performance for its client’s upstream, midstream and downstream operations

    We provide support for the full life cycle of oil & gas projects on an integrated basis in line with our clients’ requirements. Through our organization
    with strong and experienced international technical partners, we provide products, services and solutions that optimize customer performance in
    a safe and environmentally sound manner.

    Strategy

    OESS aims to double our volume of supply and operations from 2020 till 2025 fiscal year, providing unique complex solutions to our clients
    (customers and suppliers), with balanced development of our business segments resulting from an efficient use of our asset base, investment
    in technology and innovation, strategic acquisitions, continuous development of our employees and strengthening of our operations all over
    the world

    Management

    OESS implements management principles at the most fundamental level that consists of a set of general principles:

    • Sustainable business reputation,
    • Professional team of leaders,
    • Financial stability

    Our Values

    Teamwork

    We treat each other with respect and communicate openly. We foster collaboration while encouraging individual contribution and responsibility. We encourage the best ideas to surface from anywhere within the organization. We appreciate the value of multiple perspectives and aim for a values-driven culture based on integrity, respect and teamwork.

    Environment

    We are committed to environmentally sustainable policies and practices within our area of interest. Environmental concerns are essential to our decision making, and we’re committed to helping everyone in our company understand the value of acting in an environmentally responsible way.


    We address most urgent environmental challenges by targeting issues that affect people around the world. We strive to minimize our negative impact on the environment. We support sustainable and safe methods of operations that reduce environmental degradation, maintain the productivity of the land over time, and support the economic viability

    Integrity and Trust

    We are honest with ourselves and others. We meet the highest ethical standards in all our business dealings. We do what we say and always keep our word. We accept responsibility and hold ourselves accountable for our work and our actions. We encourage a culture of zero tolerance to fraud and corruption and are responsible corporate citizens. Trust is the basis for everything we do and the prerequisite for our suc- cess. We keep our promises to all our employees and all stakeholders in OESS (such as customers, employees, investors, business partners and society). We are aware that trust is hard to gain and can be easily destroyed.

    Continuous Improvement

    We are committed to excellence in everything we do and continually strive to improve across the organization. We are passionate about achieving results that exceed expectations our own and those of others. We drive for positive results with energy and a sense of urgency.
